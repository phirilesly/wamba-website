About Us

Imagine walking into a living space that looks clean, fresh, and with colors that perfectly fit your aesthetic. At Wamba Projects, we want to make this dream a reality. Our team of painters will show up on time and provide you with the quality you expect in a timeline that's unexpected! We can guarantee that we'll leave you saying WOW!

We work with the best materials and have the experience to take on a roofing job on any type of home, regardless of the complexity of the architecture. We’re insured, certified, bonded and have more than 1,000 projects behind us and plenty of glowing reviews that stand as a testament to our approach to quality.

Customer satisfaction is what our company strives for and is our number one goal. We help our clients make their homes more enjoyable for their families while getting the most value out of their properties. We work hard to continuously improve our reputation for accessibility, professionalism, performance, and the depth and quality of our long-term relationships with our clients. It is what Wamba Projects does best, and distinguishes them among competitors.

We don’t just work for customers, we build relationships with them. We know that your home is one of your greatest investments therefore we use an exceptional line of products and top quality craftsmanship. We offer free estimates, easy financing is also available for qualifying customers.
